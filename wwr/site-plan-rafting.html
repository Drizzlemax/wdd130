Overview

Purpose

Rafting Wanderers is a company dedicated to providing unforgettable white-water rafting adventures on the Berg River. We combine exhilarating rapids with breathtaking scenery to create a unique and adventurous experience for all skill levels. Embark on a journey of discovery and exploration with us, and create lasting memories on the water!

Audience

Calling all adventurous families! Embark on an unforgettable rafting adventure down the Berg River with Rafting Wanderers. Our experienced guides will ensure a safe and thrilling experience for all ages. Create lasting memories as you paddle through exciting rapids and soak in the breathtaking beauty of the Western Cape.

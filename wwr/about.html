WELCOME TO WHITE WATER WANDERERS

Conquer the currents of the Berg River with White Water Wanderers! We offer exhilarating white water rafting adventures for all skill levels, showcasing the breathtaking scenery of the Western Cape.

History

Born on the Berg River:
The story of White Water Wanderers began in August 2017, sparked by a thrilling white-water rafting competition in Paarl. Desmond Chibanda, visiting a college friend, was instantly captivated by the energy and beauty of the Berg River. This experience ignited a passion, and Desmond knew he had to share it. Convincing Anton Byers, a resident entrepreneur and family friend of his college mate, wasn't difficult. Anton, with his experience and enthusiasm, was the perfect partner for this adventure. Together, Desmond and Anton embarked on a mission to create Rafting Wanderers, a company dedicated to sharing the magic of the Berg River with like-minded adventurers.

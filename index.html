Harare, Zimbabwe

Zimbabwe, a land of wonders, beckons travelers with its diverse landscapes and rich history. Explore the Victoria Falls, one of the Seven Natural Wonders of the World, where the Zambezi River plunges dramatically into the Bakota Gorge. Embark on a safari adventure in Hwange National Park, encounter majestic elephants, and witness untamed wildlife. Step back in time at the Great Zimbabwe ruins, remnants of the medieval Kingdom of Zimbabwe. Discover the scenic Matobo Hills and the breathtaking Eastern Highlands. With a temperate climate and 365 days of sunshine, Zimbabwe invites you to explore its captivating corner of Africa!
